## Key Corrections (Apply Throughout Guide)

- **Blazing Fortress was REMOVED** — replaced entirely by Crimson Isle. Do not reference it.
- **The Park was revamped into Galatea** in 0.23 (beta) / June 2025 (AHN). The Park still exists as the first foraging island; Galatea is the second. They are distinct locations — do not conflate them.
- **Silva Dominus is a COMBAT tool** — kills Galatea mobs. It is NOT a foraging tool.
- **Mangrove trees require 50 Sweep**, NOT Foraging 40 or SkyBlock Level 120.
- **Fig Fortune and Mangrove Fortune are separate stats** from Foraging Fortune.
- **Commissions give NO coins** — rewards are HotM XP, powder (Mithril/Gemstone/Glacite), and Mining XP only.
- **Mithril Drills are NOT worth it for Crystal Hollows** — Titanium Drills are the first real upgrade.
- **Ruby Drill TX-15 and Pickonimbus 2000 both work at exactly HotM 4**.
- **Crops do NOT auto-replant in Garden** without the Replenish enchantment. Exception: pumpkins, melons, sugar cane, and mushrooms regrow from their stem blocks without Replenish.
- **Rift is NOT a primary grind target** — ~3 visits/day, ~30 min Rift Time per day. Rift Berberis income ~7–14M/hr but gated behind significant questline progression.

## 3. Galatea (Moonglade Marsh)

**Access:** From The Park (Moonglade Marsh entrance). Requires Foraging XII (12).
**Type:** Foraging island (second foraging island)
**Travel:** Travel Scroll to Galatea, Travel Scroll to Murkwater Loch
**Fairy Souls:** 12
**Resources:** Fig Log, Tender Wood, Mangrove Log, Vinesap, Lushlilac, Sea Lumies

### Overview
Galatea is an archipelago; only the first island (Moonglade Marsh) is currently released. Two more islands (Torrhus Canyon, Lunarise) are planned but not yet available.

### Core Systems

**Heart of the Forest (HotF)** — Galatea's equivalent of HotM. Leveled via Tree Gifts and Agatha Contests. Grants Tokens of the Forest for unlocking perks. Access via the Heart of the Forest item.

**Tree Gifts** — Loot dropped when a tree is fully cut. Rewards scale with % of tree cut by the player (10% = Gift, 20% = +50% rewards, 33% = +100% rewards). Encourages cooperative chopping.

**Tree Styles / Sweep** — Trees have a "toughness" level. Must cut in correct order (Trunk → Branches for Fig; Branches → Trunk → Roots for Mangrove). Sweep stat must exceed tree toughness or you take a 50% Sweep reduction penalty.
- Fig Tree toughness: 7
- Mangrove Tree toughness: **50 Sweep required** (NOT Foraging 40 or SkyBlock Level 120)

**Hunting** — New mechanic in Galatea. Use Lassos and Huntraps to capture mobs. Taught via Auryon (Evergreen Plateau) and Dax (Moonglade's Edge).

**Agatha's Contests** — Agatha (Murkwater Loch) runs contests for Foraging XP and HotF XP.

## 5. Dwarven Mines

**Access:** Bottom of Deep Caverns (Obsidian Sanctuary). Requires Mining XII + Rhys's quest (15 enchanted ore).
**Type:** Mining location
**Travel:** Travel Scroll to Dwarven Mines
**Fairy Souls:** 11

### Overview
Primary focuses: completing Commissions, leveling Heart of the Mountain (HotM), and collecting Mithril Powder, Gemstone Powder, and Glacite Powder.

### Navigation
Players arrive at Dwarven Village. Left path → Royal Palace via Palace Bridge (overlooks Royal Mines). Right path → rest of Dwarven Mines via Gates to the Mines (overlooks The Forge).

**Dwarven Mines Rail System** — Station Master in Dwarven Village; Ticket Masters at stations. Pay small coin fee to ride minecarts to other areas.

**Crystal Hollows Gate** — Gwendolyn at outskirts of Dwarven Village. Requires HotM IV. Pay 10,000 coins for a 6-hour pass. After completing Dulin's quest (HotM VII), access is permanent and free.

**Dwarven Base Camp** — Beyond Great Ice Wall. Requires HotM VII + Dulin's quest.

### Key NPCs
- **King** — issues Commissions
- **Emissaries** (Carlton, Ceanna, Wilson, Lilith, Fraiser, Eliza, Braum) — Commission check-in NPCs at various locations; unlocked progressively via Commission milestones
- **Bubu** — sells pickaxes
- **Bulvar** — sells Tier 12 mining minions
- **Banker Broadjaw** — bank access
- **Fragilis** — Handy Block Guide
- **Geo** — add Gemstones to items
- **Gwendolyn** — Crystal Hollows pass
- **Station Master / Ticket Masters** — rail travel
- **Fetchur** — daily item request for rewards
- **Forger / The Forge** — forge items using Mithril/Titanium

### Commissions
Commissions are tasks from the King. **They give NO coins.** Rewards are:
- Mining XP: 5–10k (Dwarven Mines), 10–20k (Crystal Hollows)
- HotM XP: 100 at HotM 1, 200 at HotM 2, 400 at HotM 3+, 0 at HotM 10
- Powder: 500 Mithril Powder (Dwarven Mines) / 1,000 Gemstone Powder (Crystal Hollows)
- At HotM 10: 1,000 Mithril Powder / 2,000 Gemstone Powder instead

**Daily Bonus:** First 4 commissions each day grant +900 HotM XP (until HotM 10 max). After HotM 10, daily commissions give +1,600 Mithril / +3,200 Gemstone / +4,000 Glacite Powder instead.

**Commission Milestones:**
- 5 commissions → unlock additional Emissaries
- 25 commissions → unlock more Emissaries
- 100 commissions → 3rd Commission slot
- 250 commissions → Travel Scroll to Dwarven Mines
- 500 commissions → Royal Pigeon (fast travel within Dwarven Mines)
- 750 commissions → Travel Scroll to Crystal Nucleus

### Events (Every ~20 Minutes)

| Event | Type | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 2x Powder | Passive | All powder doubled |
| Gone with the Wind | Passive | Mine in wind direction → faster Mining Speed |
| Better Together | Passive | Near other players → bonus Mining Speed and Mining Fortune |
| Mithril Gourmand | Active | Mine Tasty Mithril, hand in 200 to Don Expresso |
| Raffle | Active | Collect Raffle Tickets, deposit to Raffle Box |
| Goblin Raid | Active | Defeat goblin hordes in 5 minutes |
| Fallen Star | Minor | Random location drop zone; bonus Mithril/Titanium/Starfall; Star Sentries spawn |
| Powder Ghast | Minor | Shoot Powder Ghast to earn Mithril Powder |

### Recommended Progression
- HotM 1-3: Dwarven Mines commissions only (100 and 200 HotM XP each)
- HotM 3+: 400 HotM XP per commission — efficient to push Crystal Hollows
- HotM 4: Unlock Crystal Hollows access (10k coin pass)
- HotM 5: Peak of the Mountain perk → 4th Commission slot
- HotM 7: Permanent free Crystal Hollows access + Dwarven Base Camp

### Resources
Mithril, Titanium, Tasty Mithril, standard ores. Goblins drop Goblin Eggs, Goblin Armor. Ghosts drop Ghostly Boots, Plasma, Volta, Sorrow.

## 6. Crystal Hollows

**Access:** Via Gwendolyn in Dwarven Village. Requires Heart of the Mountain IV.
Cost: 10,000 coins for 6-hour pass. Permanent free access after HotM VII + Dulin's quest.
**Type:** Mining location
**Fairy Souls:** Scattered throughout

### Overview
Primary focuses: mining Gemstones, collecting Gemstone Crystals, completing the Crystal Nucleus. The terrain is randomly generated each server instance (structures change location per lobby, tunnels remain the same).

Unlike other areas, **broken blocks are NOT replaced with bedrock** — they physically become air. Blocks can be placed (Torches, Ladders, Hard Stone).

### Five Main Areas

| Area | Gemstone | Sub-Location | Crystal Task |
|---|---|---|---|
| Jungle | Amethyst | Jungle Temple | Obtain Jungle Key → complete parkour |
| Mithril Deposits | Jade | Mines of Divan | Use Metal Detector → find Scavenged Items |
| Precursor Remnants | Sapphire | Lost Precursor City | Collect 6 Automaton Parts (or Precursor Apparatus) |
| Goblin Holdout | Amber | Goblin Queen's Den | Give any Goblin Egg to King Yolkar without being caught |
| Magma Fields | Topaz | Khazad-dûm | Defeat Bal |

Ruby Gemstones spawn throughout the entire Crystal Hollows (not zone-specific).
Jasper spawns only in Fairy Grottos (3 spawn per lobby in random locations).

### Crystal Nucleus
Place all 5 Gemstone Crystals at the Nucleus to complete a "nucleus run." Crystals can alternatively be used (while "Not Placed") in The Forge to craft Perfect Gemstones.

Crystal status visible in tab list: Not Found (red) / Not Placed (yellow) / Placed (green).

### Treasure Chests
- Spawn randomly when mining; must hit critical particles 5 times to open; last ~1 minute
- Loot Chests at fixed locations within structures; same loot for all players in same lobby instance
- 5% chance for any chest to be a rare chest (better loot)
- Crystal Serendipity attribute: up to +20% chance to double chest rewards

### Key NPCs
- **Geonathan Greatforge** — forging hub within Crystal Hollows
- **Odawa** — quests
- **King Yolkar** — Goblin Queen's Den (Amber Crystal)
- **Professor Robot** — Lost Precursor City (Automaton Parts)

### Key Drops
Gemstones (all types), Goblin Eggs, Bal Pet, Scatha Pet, Worm Membrane, Divan Fragments, Helix, Golden Dragon Egg, Pickonimbus 2000, Jaderald, Prehistoric Egg, Divan's Alloy, Wishing Compass

### Gear Notes
- **Mithril Drills are NOT worth it for Crystal Hollows** — too slow for gemstone mining.
- **Titanium Drills are the first real upgrade** for Crystal Hollows.
- **Ruby Drill TX-15 and Pickonimbus 2000 both require exactly HotM 4** — no additional HotM investment needed beyond entry requirement.
- Mithril Powder removed from Crystal Hollows chests (as of 0.20.6 / Aug 2024).

### Ore Yield Boosts (Crystal Hollows)
| Ore | Base Yield |
|---|---|
| Coal Ore | 4 Coal |
| Lapis Ore | 9 Lapis |
| Redstone Ore | 22 Redstone |
| Emerald Ore | 4 Emerald |
| Diamond Ore | 4 Diamond |

**Type:** Combat island (harder than The End)
**Travel:** Multiple Travel Scrolls available (to Crimson Isle, Kuudra Passage, Smoldering Tomb, Dragontail, Scarleton, Kuudra Skull)

**Note:** **Blazing Fortress was REMOVED** and replaced by Crimson Isle. Do not reference Blazing Fortress.

Gain reputation by killing mobs or doing quests (including Elle's questline). Reputation unlocks faction-exclusive items and features. Guards in settlements are passive until attacked. Barbarian Emissary and Mage Emissary serve as Commission check-in NPCs.

## 9. The Rift Dimension

**Access:** Wizard Portal in Hub Wilderness (by the mountain). Requires SkyBlock Level 12.
**Type:** Separate dimension with unique mechanics
**Currency:** Motes (not coins)
**Fairy Souls equivalent:** Enigma Souls

### Dimensional Infusion (Entry System)
Three ways to enter:
1. Pay Wizard 32 Grand Experience Bottles
2. Pay Wizard 200 Bits (can toggle Auto-Buy)
3. Have a Rift Prism in inventory/Accessory Bag, or imbued via Erihann

**Free Infusions:** 1 free every 4 hours; up to 3 stored. An Infusion is consumed upon leaving the Wizard Tower (Rift), not upon entering. If you join the same Rift instance 3 times with one Infusion, it is consumed.

### Important Restrictions
- **NOT a primary grind target** — approximately 3 visits/day, ~30 min Rift Time per day for typical players
- Items, Stats, and Potion effects do NOT transfer between dimensions (exception: Rift Transferable and Rift Exportable items)
- Rift-only stats: Rift Time, Rift Damage, Speed, Intelligence, Magic Find, Magic Resistance, Hearts

### Rift Time Mechanic
- Base Rift Time: 480 seconds (8 minutes)
- Each Rift Time point = 1 second in the Rift
- Timer pauses inside Wizard Tower (Rift, except bottom floor), Rift Gallery, or Mirrorverse
- Timer runs at half speed near Colosseum
- Warning at 10s remaining; last 10s immune to time penalties
- When timer hits 0 → teleported back to Hub Wizard Tower

**Increasing Rift Time:**
| Method | Max Bonus |
|---|---|
| McGrubber's Burger (eat up to 5) | +60s |
| Reed questline (steps 1–5) | +130s |
| Porhtal's Eyes | +280s |
| Vampire Slayer levels (1–5) | +235s |
| Sun Gecko Essence Shop (Blessing of Time) | +90s |
| Spotlite (eat up to 5) | +125s |

### Locations
9 main zones: Wyld Woods, Black Lagoon, West Village, Dreadfarm, Village Plaza, Living Cave, Colosseum (Rift), Stillgore Chateau, The Mountaintop

**Stillgore Chateau** → unlocks Vampire Slayer

### Motes Currency
- Motes Orbs spawn throughout the Rift: collect by walking through them (+25 Motes, +2 Rift Time each; +10 Motes only in Enigma's Crib)
- Max 100 orbs at once; no orb within 20-block radius of another
- Other Motes sources: killing mobs, Motes Grubber NPC (selling items), completing bosses

### Berberis Income
- Rift Berberis farming: **~7–14M coins/hr** but gated behind significant Rift questline progression
- Must complete major portions of the Rift questline before this income is accessible

## 12. The Garden

**Access:** Via SkyBlock Menu. Requires SkyBlock Level 5. Each player has their own private Garden.
**Type:** Personal Farming island
**Key NPCs:** Anita, Carpenter, Jacob, Jeff, Sam, Pesthunter Phillip, Pesthunter Pamela
**Plots:** 24 total in a 5x5 grid; Barn in the center

### Overview
Like the Private Island, each player has their own Garden. It has 15 levels, leveled with Garden XP from Crop Milestones and accepting Visitor offers. Each Garden level gives +10 SkyBlock XP, unlocks new Visitors, barn skins, crops, crop upgrades, and faster crop growth.

### Core Mechanics

**Crops (unlock order by Garden level):**
Wheat (1), Carrot (2), Potato (3), Pumpkin (4), Sugar Cane (5), Melon (6), Cactus (7), Cocoa Beans (8), Mushroom (9), Nether Wart (10), Sunflower (11), Moonflower (11), Wild Rose (12)

**Crop Growth:**
- No light required (since 0.18.3), but water IS required
- Dry soil = no growth at all
- Not fully moist soil = -40% growth speed debuff

**Auto-Replant:**
- **Crops do NOT auto-replant** without the Replenish enchantment
- **Exception:** Pumpkins, melons, and sugar cane regrow from stem/base blocks. Mushrooms also regrow from mycelium under some conditions.

**Plots:**
- 24 plots, each 96x96 blocks
- Unlock with Compost (or Compost Bundles for later plots) at The Desk
- Garden Level requirements: Group A (level 1), B (level 3), C (level 5), D (level 7)
- Each plot gives +3 Farming Fortune and +5 SkyBlock XP
- Plots must be cleaned before use; Sam's Scythe or Garden Scythe speeds this up

**Composter:**
- Converts crops and Organic Matter into Compost
- Requires Machine Fuel (e.g., Biofuel)
- 1 Compost per 10 minutes; requires 4,000 Organic Matter + 2,000 Fuel

**The Desk** (in Barn, or `/desk`):
Configure plots, add Crop Upgrades, access SkyMart, view Crop Milestones, change Barn Skin

### Visitors
NPCs that periodically visit the Garden and offer trades for Farming XP, Garden XP, Copper, Bits, and unique rewards.

- Base interval: 15 minutes between visitors
- 50+ unique visitors served: reduced to 12 minutes
- 100+ unique visitors served: reduced to 9 minutes
- Max 5 visitors on Garden at once (6th queues hidden)
- 137 unique visitors total; each served visitor grants +1 Farming Fortune via Green Thumb enchantment
- Visitor rarity affects how often they appear; Fancy Visit accessory boosts rare visitor chance

### Crop Upgrades
Increase Farming Fortune for a specific crop. Purchased with Copper at The Desk. Each upgrade = +5 Farming Fortune for that crop.

| Tier | Garden Level | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| I | 2 | 5 Copper |
| II | 4 | 10 Copper |
| III | 6 | 20 Copper |
| IV | 8 | 40 Copper |
| V | 10 | 80 Copper |

(Additional tiers up to IX at level 15.)

### Pests
Mobs that spawn when crops are broken in the Garden. 10 pest types unlocked progressively. When 4+ pests present, player's Farming Fortune decreases (up to -8 FF at 8 pests). Capture pests to restore Fortune, or exchange to Pesthunter Phillip for temporary FF bonus.

### Garden Milestones
Per-crop milestones that grant Garden XP. Primary source of Garden leveling alongside Visitor trades.

### Garden Level Milestones Summary
- Level 5: 78 Visitors, 5 crops, 6 pests, Tier I-II Crop Upgrades, Medieval Barn Skin, +40% Crop Growth
- Level 10: 120 Visitors, 10 crops, 11 pests, Tier I-V Crop Upgrades, 3 Barn Skins, +90% Crop Growth
- Level 15 (max): 137 Visitors, 13 crops, 14 pests, Tier I-IX Crop Upgrades, 5 Barn Skins, +140% Crop Growth

## 13. Jerry's Workshop (Winter Island)

**Access:** SkyBlock Menu, Jerry NPC on your island, or Event Stand in Village. Seasonal — open during all of Late Winter (~11 hours every 5 days 4 hours) and all of December (real-world). Bonus events: 3-hour window only (1 hr before to 1 hr after the event).
**Type:** Seasonal event island
**Fairy Souls:** 5
**Only location for Winter Sea Creatures fishing**

### Sublocations

| Zone | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Einary's Emporium | Winter rewards shop (North Stars and other items) |
| Gary's Shack | Gary NPC |
| Glacial Cave | Ice collection; buried treasure |
| Hot Springs | Winter Sea Creatures fishing |
| Jerry Pond | Ice collection; Winter Sea Creatures fishing |
| Mount Jerry | Season of Jerry event (erupts with lava, Magma Cubes) |
| Reflective Pond | Ice collection; fishing; Frozen Alex NPC |
| Sherry's Showroom | Sherry's item shop |
| Sunken Jerry Pond | Ice collection; fishing |
| Terry's Shack | Special Reforge Stones for Fishing Rods (Terry NPC) |

### Key NPCs
- **Jerry** — island NPC
- **St. Jerry** — rewards all 20 White Gifts → Green Gift
- **Sherry** — shop
- **Gulliver** — Chicken Race
- **Frosty** — gives Snow Cannon; near entrance
- **Gary** — Gary's Shack
- **Banker Barry** — bank access
- **Einary** — Einary's Emporium (North Stars shop)

### Events/Activities

**Defend Jerry's Workshop** (Starts 23rd Late Winter)
- Waves of mobs attack 5 Gift Piles in the village
- Mobs can only be damaged by snowballs (not regular attacks)
- 5 waves, 1 hour total duration
- Defeat Magma Cubes to end each wave; remaining Gift Piles can be looted

**Snow Cannons**
- 4 Snow Cannons at strategic positions around the map
- One player per cannon; broadcasts to lobby when mounted
- Launch snowballs for heavy damage during Defend Jerry's Workshop

**Chicken Race** (Gulliver)
- Fly a chicken through sky hoops; race ends if timer runs out or you dismount
- Cluckpoints grant first-time rewards and extend timer
- Rewards: Red Gift, Gift Compass, Frozen Chicken (upgrades to Fried Frozen Chicken), Jingle Bells

**White Gifts**
- 20 White Gifts hidden around the island each time it opens
- Locations change between SkyBlock years
- Gift Compass points to nearest undiscovered gift
- Collect all 20 → St. Jerry rewards Green Gift

### Key Items
- **North Stars** — primary Winter currency; spent at Einary's Emporium
- **Gift types** — White, Green, Red; various reward tiers
- **Winter Sea Creatures** — unique fish only catchable here
- **Ice** — collectible resource from ponds and Glacial Cave

### Notes
- Only location where Winter Sea Creatures can be fished
- Island is entirely unavailable outside of Late Winter and December (real-time)
- During bonus events only, island opens for 3 hours only (1 hr before/after event)
