# SkyBlock Skills & Collections — Research Reference

Generated: 2026-03-04. Sources: `leveling.json` (NEU repo), `collections_resource.json` (Hypixel API v2), local wiki dumps.

---

## Important Corrections

- **Bazaar purchases do NOT count toward collections.** Materials must be gathered manually. This corrects a common misconception and has been confirmed multiple times.
- **Foraging max level is 54**, not 50. Levels 51–54 require specific collection unlocks and NPC purchases (see Foraging section).
- **Farming and Taming default cap is 50** from the XP table, but both can be raised to 60 via in-game progression (Jacob's Contests / George upgrades).
- **Hunting is a real skill** (added June 2025), max level 25. Uses its own XP system (shard rarity-based).
- **Lapis Armor gives +200% XP from mining ores only** — not general skill XP, not mob kill XP.
- **Fairy Souls no longer give stats** — they only award SkyBlock XP now.

## Skills — Per-Skill Breakdown

### Combat (max 60)

**Stat bonuses per level:** +0.5% Crit Chance, +4% damage dealt to mobs (every level)
**Cumulative at max (60):** +30% Crit Chance, +210% damage to mobs

**Training methods (best to worst):**
1. Slayer bosses — high XP per kill, best for late-game
2. Dungeons mobs — good XP, rewards Dungeoneering separately
3. Bestiary milestones — passive bonus XP for kill counts
4. Arachne's Burrow (Spider's Den) — good early-game spot with respawning spiders
5. Endermen at The End — strong mid-game option
6. Combat minion collection (reduced XP vs. direct kills)

**Notes:**
- No XP from killing mobs on Private Island
- Combat Wisdom stat (from gear/pets) multiplies XP gained
- Useful pets: Wolf Pet (Combat XP boost)
- Level 1 unlocks Spider's Den access

---

### Farming (max 60)

**Default cap: 50.** Raised to 60 by purchasing upgrades from Anita (Farmhouse) after placing Gold bracket in Jacob's Farming Contests for each unique crop type — 10 crops = +10 levels to reach 60.

**Stat bonuses per level:**
- +4 HP per level (cumulative +240 at 60)
- +4 Farming Fortune per level (cumulative +240 at 60 — enables triple/quadruple drops)

**Training methods:**
1. Garden (primary method) — requires SkyBlock Level 5 to unlock via Sam. Far more efficient than public islands due to Farming Fortune being disabled on Private Island
2. Naturally grown crops on public islands (The Barn, Mushroom Desert, Hub Farm) — intro-level only
3. Farming minions — collect from Wheat, Carrot, Potato, Melon, Pumpkin, Nether Wart, etc. minions
4. Jacob's Farming Contests — periodic crop-specific contests, also required to raise level cap

**Key crops by XP yield (base):** Wheat +4, Carrot +3, Potato +3, Melon Slice +2, Sugar Cane +3, Pumpkin +4, Nether Wart +1, Cactus +2

**Notes:**
- Garden plots require Compost to unlock; unlocked at Garden levels
- Pests mechanic at Garden 5 — pests reduce Farming Fortune but drop loot; kill with Vacuums
- Greenhouse available at Garden 7 — slower growth but increased drops and Mutations
- Replenish enchant (from Cocoa Beans T8 collection) prevents replanting

### Mining (max 60)

**Stat bonuses per level:** +2 Defense per level, +2 Mining Fortune per level
**Cumulative at max (60):** +106 Defense, +240 Mining Fortune (triple/quadruple ore drops)

**Training methods:**
1. Dwarven Mines — Mithril, Titanium, Gemstones yield high XP; best mid-game
2. Crystal Hollows — highest XP ores (Diamond, Emerald, Gemstones); endgame mining
3. Commissions (Dwarven Mines) — awarded via NPC tasks, efficient supplemental XP
4. Deep Caverns — good early game (Lapis, Redstone, Diamond)
5. Coal/Iron/Gold in Gold Mine — beginner zone
6. Mining minions — Cobblestone, Iron, Gold, Coal, Lapis (reduced XP)

**XP per ore (sample):** Cobblestone +1, Coal Ore +5, Iron Ore +5, Gold Ore +6, Lapis Ore +7, Redstone Ore +7, Emerald Ore +9, Diamond Ore +10, Dwarven Gold +20

**Notes:**
- Lapis Armor gives +200% XP from mining ores specifically — not global skill XP
- Heart of the Mountain (HOTM) progression is separate from Mining skill
- Snow Minion grants Mining XP from collection

### Foraging (max 54)

**Default cap: 50** from XP table. Levels 51–54 unlocked via:
- Fig Log Collection T9 (150,000 Fig Logs) → +1 level
- Mangrove Log Collection T9 (150,000 Mangrove Logs) → +1 level
- Agatha's Milestone 4 → purchase 2 more levels from Agatha's shop

**Stat bonuses per level:** +2 Strength per level, +4 Foraging Fortune per level
**Cumulative at max (54):** +86 Strength (confirmed from wiki maxed stats), +200 Foraging Fortune (triple drops from logs)

**Training methods:**
1. Fig Log (Galatea) — +15 XP per log, best rate
2. Mangrove Log (Galatea) — +20 XP per log, highest single-item rate
3. Dark Oak / Oak / Birch / Spruce / Jungle / Acacia logs — +6 XP each (The Park, Savanna, etc.)
4. Flowers — +1 XP each (Flower House, Park areas)
5. Foraging minions (Oak, Birch, Jungle, Dark Oak, Flower) — reduced XP

**Notes:**
- The Park is the standard early-game foraging zone
- Galatea logs give significantly more XP and should be targeted once accessible
- Ocelot Pet boosts Foraging XP

### Fishing (max 60)

**Default cap listed in leveling.json as 50**, but the wiki and skill page confirm Fishing goes to 60. The API caps entry may be outdated; the wiki skill page shows level 60 rewards.

**Stat bonuses per level:** +4 HP per level, +1 Treasure Chance per level (up to +10% at level 60 from Fishing alone per wiki maxed stats)

**Training methods:**
1. Fishing in Jerry's Workshop (Winter event) — fast sea creature spawns
2. Magma fields / lava fishing (Crimson Isle) — mid-to-late game
3. Trophy fishing spots — high value sea creatures, good XP
4. Open water fishing (Hub, Spider's Den, Mushroom Desert) — early game
5. Fishing minions / Clay minions — reduced XP collection

**Notes:**
- Fishing Speed reduces wait time between catches (lower = faster)
- Dolphin Pet scales based on nearby players
- Sea creature kills also grant Fishing XP
- Squid Pet boosts Fishing XP

### Enchanting (max 60)

**Stat bonuses per level:** +2 Intelligence per level, +0.5 Ability Damage per level
**Cumulative at max (60):** +106 Intelligence, +30 Ability Damage, +300% XP gained from all sources

**Training methods:**
1. Experimentation Table — most XP per session; minigame-based, has a cooldown
2. Enchanting Table — XP scales as 3.5×(Minecraft levels spent)^1.5; spend max levels for efficiency
3. Anvil (adding enchantment books to gear) — reasonable XP but costly in books
4. Fishing up Experience Bottles, Grand Experience Bottles, Titanic Experience Bottles

**Notes:**
- Combining two books does NOT give Enchanting XP — only applying books to gear counts
- Guardian Pet boosts Enchanting XP; recommended at Rare+ rarity
- XP formula per Minecraft level spent: `3.5 × X^1.5` where X = levels spent

### Alchemy (max 60)

**Default cap in leveling.json: 50.** The wiki skill page and Taming page both confirm Alchemy can go to 60 in-game — leveling.json cap data appears to reflect older/default values. Treat max 60 as target.

**Stat bonuses per level:** +2 Intelligence per level
**Cumulative at max (60):** +86 Intelligence, +50% potion duration (Brewer ability: +1% duration per level)

**Training methods:**
1. Enchanted Blaze Rod (Strength potions) — best XP rate, expensive
2. Enchanted Sugar Cane (Speed potions) — good XP, very accessible
3. Enchanted Sugar (Speed potions) — ~3x more efficient per item than Enchanted Sugar Cane but slower brewing
4. Enchanted Melon (Regeneration) — best XP-to-coin ratio for budget players; ~0.97 XP per coin
5. Nether Wart minions — basic farming for Awkward Potions

**Notes:**
- XP collected when taking potion out of Brewing Stand, not when brewing starts
- Alchemy XP Boost Potions (from Gifts in Jerry's Workshop) — save materials, then drink boost
- Cheap/Decent Coffee can substitute Awkward Potion base for faster brews

### Taming (max 60)

**Default cap in leveling.json: 50.** Raised to 60 by donating specific pets to George (pets are consumed permanently):
Rift Ferret (Epic), Slug (Epic), Spirit (Epic), Giraffe (Epic), Jellyfish (Epic), Bal (Epic), Baby Yeti (Epic), Black Cat (Legendary), Frost Wisp (Rare), Enderman (Mythic)

**Stat bonuses per level:** +1% Pet XP gained (Zoologist ability) and +1 Pet Luck per level
**Cumulative at max (60):** +60% Pet XP, +60 Pet Luck (base is 20%, so effective Pet Luck is 30% at Taming 50; note: bonus is divided by 5)

**At level 25:** +25% Pet XP, +25 Pet Luck
**At level 50:** +50% Pet XP, +50 Pet Luck

**Kat upgrade requirements (Taming level gates):**
- Rare → Epic: Taming 10
- Epic → Legendary: Taming 20
- Legendary → Mythic: Taming 25

**Training methods:**
- Taming XP comes from pet XP gain — any skill that earns XP while a pet is equipped contributes
- Pet type matching: using a pet matching the skill XP type grants full Taming XP; mismatched type gives less
- Level 100 pets still grant Taming XP (excess credited to pet for rarity upgrades)

### Carpentry (max 60)

**Default cap in leveling.json: 50.** Wiki confirms max is 60.

**Stat bonuses per level:** +1 HP per level (approximately — cumulative is 49 HP at level 50 from wiki)
**Additional rewards:** Furniture recipe unlocks

**Training methods:**
- Craft items — XP = 3% of combined NPC sell price of ingredients
- Most vanilla recipes do NOT grant Carpentry XP; use SkyBlock recipes
- Sapphire Power Scrolls — good XP and can be sold on Auction House for profit
- Use skyblocktools.dev/carpentry-xp-calculator for efficient craft planning

**Unlock requirement:** Give the Carpenter NPC 64 Wool to unlock the Carpentry Table recipe.

**Notes:**
- Quick Crafting (instant craft if materials in inventory) unlocks at Carpentry 3; requires VIP+ rank (all players on Bingo profiles)
- Carpentry is non-cosmetic and contributes to skill average

### Social (max 25, cosmetic)

Social is a **cosmetic skill** — does not affect skill average.

**No stat bonuses.** Rewards are cosmetic unlocks and Amelia's Shop access.

**Training methods:**
- 10 XP per unique guest visiting your island
- 2 XP per minute with guests on your island
- 1 XP per minute while visiting other players' islands

**Total XP to max (25):** 272,800

### Runecrafting (max 25, cosmetic)

Runecrafting is a **cosmetic skill** — does not affect skill average. Unranked players hard-cap at level 3.

**No stat bonuses.** Provides access to higher-level runes.

**Training methods:**
1. Kill Runic Mobs (purple-named, 4x HP, 2x damage, guaranteed rune drop)
2. Fuse runes at Dusk's Runic Pedestal (XP on success and failure; more on success)
3. Kill certain Bosses (Ender Dragon drops runes)

**Rank XP multipliers:** Default ×1.0, VIP ×1.1, VIP+ ×1.25, MVP ×1.5, MVP+ ×2.0, MVP++ ×3.0

**Total XP to max (25):** 94,300

### Hunting (max 25)

Added June 2025. Hunting is **not cosmetic** — it grants Hunter Fortune and increases shard charm chance.

**Stat bonuses per level:** +1 Hunter Fortune per level (25% chance to double shards at max)
**At max (25):** +1% chance to charm mobs and capture their Attribute Shard

**Training methods:**
1. Forest Hunting — use Abysmal Lasso or better on Forest Attribute mobs; deplete stamina bar until REEL appears
2. Water Hunting — use Basic Fishing Net or better on Water Attribute mobs
3. Combat Hunting — use Worn Huntaxe - Genesis + Small Pocket Black Hole on mobs; deal damage until <1 HP, then absorb
4. Fusing Attribute Shards — contributes XP
5. David Hunterborough's Trap Hunts and Salt Hunts milestone rewards
6. Collection milestones: Lushlilac T4, Tender Wood T8, Vinesap T8

**XP from shard rarity:**
| Shard Rarity | Hunting XP |
|--------------|------------|
| Common       | 75         |
| Uncommon     | 150        |
| Rare         | 300        |
| Epic         | 500        |
| Legendary    | 1,000      |

**Note:** Hunting uses the **standard leveling_xp table** (same as other skills), not a custom table.
