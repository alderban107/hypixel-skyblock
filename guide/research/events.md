## 1. Spooky Festival

**Timing:** Autumn 29-31 every SkyBlock year (every ~11 hours 40 minutes real time, lasts ~1 hour)

Foxy's Extra Event perk can schedule bonus Spooky Festivals. Additional festivals are also run around real-life Halloween.

### Mechanics

All mobs have a **10% base chance** to drop Green Candy when killed. Green Candy has a base 10% chance to convert to Purple Candy on drop, further modified by `ConvertChance + MobLevel/5`.

**Boosting Green Candy drop rate:**

| Source | Extra Chance | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Candy Talisman | +5% | Only highest tier counts |
| Candy Ring | +10% | Only highest tier counts |
| Candy Artifact | +15% | Only highest tier counts |
| Candy Relic | +20% | Only highest tier counts |
| Candied Reforge | +1% per piece | Up to +4% (all 4 armor pieces) |
| Bat Pet | +0.1% to +20% | Scales with pet level |
| Spooky Armor (full set) | +25% | 5% per piece |
| Bat Person Armor (full set) | +25% | 5% per piece |
| Foxy's Sweet Benevolence perk | +30% | Mayor perk |

**Scoring:** Green Candy = 1 point. Purple Candy = 5 points. Candy from Bat Pinatas does NOT count toward the leaderboard.

#### Spooky Mobs

Spawn at night during the festival in: Graveyard, Wilderness, Spider's Den, Obsidian Sanctuary.

#### Trick or Treat Chests

1% chance from normal mobs, 3% from Spooky Mobs. Each chest gives either a TRICK (spawns a Spooky Mob) or a TREAT (1-3 items). Lucky Hoof and Eternal Hoof accessories increase spawn chance.

When Foxy's "A Time for Giving" perk is active, Party Chests spawn instead (no mob spawns, 2-4 items, larger quantities, some unique loot).

#### Headless Horseman (Boss)

Spawned by using a **Horseman's Candle** (purchased from Fear Mongerer for 32 Purple Candy). Requires nighttime (7pm-5am) and must be used in the Graveyard or Wilderness.

- Health: 3,000,000 HP
- Drops: 0-2 Green Candy, 0-2 Purple Candy, Spooky Shards (spawner gets 4, 1st place gets 1, 2nd place gets 0-1), chance at Midnight Dye
- Abilities: Headless Zombie Swarm, Bat Swarm (throws Damage II and Wounded II potions)
- Cannot be damaged by arrows or Bonemerang (pulls player toward boss and blinds for 3s)
- Can spawn as Runic (10M HP instead of 12M)

### Rewards (End-of-Event Leaderboard)

| Bracket | Points Required | Rewards |
|---|---|---|
| Top 50 | Various | 1M coins + Skeleton Horse Pet + Witch Mask + Vampirism VI book + Spooky Disc + Spooky Pie + 50 Carnival Tickets |
| Emerald | 5,000 | 1M coins + Skeleton Horse Pet + Witch Mask + Vampirism VI book + Spooky Disc + Spooky Pie + 35 Carnival Tickets |
| Diamond | 2,000 | 500k coins + Witch Mask + Vampirism VI book + Spooky Disc + Spooky Pie + 25 Carnival Tickets |
| Gold | 1,750 | 200k coins + Vampirism VI book + Spooky Disc + Spooky Pie + 20 Carnival Tickets |
| Iron | 1,500 | 100k coins + Vampirism VI book + Spooky Disc + Spooky Pie + 15 Carnival Tickets |
| Stone | 1,000 | 50k coins + Spooky Disc + Spooky Pie + 10 Carnival Tickets |
| Wood | 100 | 25k coins + Spooky Pie + 5 Carnival Tickets |

Rewards can be claimed from the Fear Mongerer or from the Calendar Event Rewards stash. Unclaimed rewards expire after 10 SkyBlock years.

### Fear Mongerer Shop Items

The Fear Mongerer is present 1 hour before and 1 hour after the festival. Notable purchases:
- **Spooky Armor** (Epic): 64 Purple Candy per piece (256 total for full set). Each piece grants +5% Green Candy and +5% Purple Candy chance. Upgrades to Bat Person Armor using Spooky Shards.
- **Horseman's Candle**: 32 Purple Candy — used to spawn Headless Horseman

### Werewolf Armor

**Correction:** Werewolf Armor is crafted from **Werewolf Skins** dropped by the **Werewolf sea creature** (fished during Spooky Festival), NOT from Wolf Slayer.

- Werewolf = Epic sea creature, fished in the 3-hour window around the festival (requires Fishing 17+)
- 100% chance to drop 1 Werewolf Skin, 10% chance for 2
- Full set requires 96 Werewolf Skins (Helmet 20, Chestplate 32, Leggings 28, Boots 16)
- Set bonus: Regenerative Howl — on fervor activate, heals nearby players for 1% of your Defense and grants +50 Defense for 5s (up to 10 stacks)

### Strategy

- Priority: Maximize Purple Candy rate using Candy Relic + Spooky/Bat Person armor + Bat Pet
- Farm high-level mobs (Spiders Den, Obsidian Sanctuary) for higher mob level = higher purple candy conversion
- Summon Headless Horseman at night for Spooky Shards to craft Bat Person Armor
- For fishing: use Spooky Bait (+15% Werewolf weight), Whale/Light/Dark Bait (+25%), or Mythic Bat Pet (+25%)

## 2. Jerry's Workshop (Season of Jerry)

**Timing:**
- **Winter Island access:** Available the entire Late Winter season (~11 hours) and all of December (real-life month)
- **Season of Jerry event:** Late Winter 24-26 (lasts 1 hour, occurs every ~124 real-life hours)
- During bonus events: Winter Island is only open for 3 hours (1 hour before, event, 1 hour after)

### Mechanics

The main event is **Defend Jerry's Workshop** — 5 waves of lava-based enemies attack five Gift Piles. Magma Cubes (the biggest threat, can destroy gift piles) can only be damaged by snowballs — making it a fair fight regardless of gear. The event lasts 1 hour total.

**Accessing Jerry's Workshop:**
- Click Jerry on your Private Island
- Use the SkyBlock Menu (icon appears above Fast Travel during season)
- Click Jerry at the Event Stand in the Village
- Command: `/savethejerrys`

### Activities at Jerry's Workshop

| Activity | Description |
|---|---|
| Defend Jerry's Workshop | Main event — 5 waves of mobs. Snow Cannons on the map deal large AoE damage. |
| Chicken Race | Fly a chicken through hoops (Gulliver). Rewards: Red Gift, Gift Compass, Frozen Chicken, Jingle Bells. |
| White Gift Hunt | 20 hidden White Gifts scattered around the map each year. Talk to St. Jerry after collecting all 20 for a Green Gift. |
| Winter Fishing | Fish up Winter Sea Creatures in the Hot Springs, Jerry Pond, Reflective Pond, and Sunken Jerry Pond. |
| Glacial Cave | Mine Ice and find buried treasure. |

### Gifts System

Gifts are obtained by defending Gift Piles, mining from Glacial Cave walls, or buying from Bazaar. Both players receive the same reward when a gift is opened (right-click target player, they right-click back).

| Gift | Rarity | Notable Drops |
|---|---|---|
| White Gift | Common | Coins (1k-10k), Skill XP, Snow Minion I, minion skins, Snow Suit pieces |
| Green Gift | Uncommon | Coins (2k-20k), Skill XP up to 2500, Golden Gift, Snow Suit pieces |
| Red Gift | Rare | Coins (5k-1M), Snowman Pet, Krampus Helmet, Holly Dye |
| Party Gift | (Foxy only) | Same as Red + Party Gloves, Party Cloak, Snowman Mask, Party Top Hat, Carnival Tickets |

**North Stars:** Currency obtained when opening Gifts (White: 10% chance for 1, Green: 30% chance for 1-3, Red: 50% chance for 1-5). Spent at Einary's Emporium for exclusive winter cosmetics and items.

### Strategy

- Season of Jerry is a strong way to earn money — gifts from each wave can be sold to Bazaar
- Use Snowman Mask, Gift Talisman line accessories for extra gift drops
- Snow Suit armor and Nutcracker armor give more gifts from the event
- Reindeer Pet boosts gift rewards

## 3. Mining Fiesta

**Timing (when Cole is mayor):** Occurs 5 times per Cole term, starting Early Summer Day 1. Each lasts 7 SkyBlock days (2 hours 20 minutes real time). Total: ~11 hours 40 minutes of event time per term.

Also triggered by Foxy's Extra Event perk (Late Summer Day 22) or Mayor Jerry's Perkpocalypse rotating to Cole perks (18 hours after rotation begins).

### Features

During Mining Fiesta:
- **+75 Mining Wisdom** (global)
- **Double ore drops** from all ores
- Chance to collect **Refined Minerals** (from ores) and **Glossy Gemstones** (from gemstones)

**Refined Minerals** drop from ores in: Deep Caverns, Gold Mine, Hub, Dwarven Mines, Crystal Hollows, Glacite Mineshafts. Rate: 1/375 chance per ore (stone, netherrack, end stone excluded). Mining Fortune does NOT affect Refined Mineral drop rate.

**Glossy Gemstones** drop from gemstones in: Crystal Hollows, Glacite Tunnels, Glacite Mineshafts. Rate: 1/725 per gemstone.

### Event Items

| Item | Rarity | Use | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Refined Mineral | Rare | Craft Mineral Armor and Mineral Talisman | 1/375 from ores |
| Glossy Gemstone | Epic | Craft Glossy Mineral Armor and Glossy Mineral Talisman | 1/725 from gemstones |
| Mineral Talisman | Rare | +3 Mining Fortune | Crafted from Refined Minerals |
| Glossy Mineral Talisman | Epic | +6 Mining Fortune | Crafted from Glossy Gemstones |
| Mineral Armor (each piece) | Epic | Mine 2 extra adjacent blocks (Breaking Power ≤3) | Crafted (requires Hardened Diamond armor + Refined Minerals) |
| Glossy Mineral Armor (each piece) | Legendary | Same as Mineral + +50 Mining Fortune on non-ore blocks per piece | Crafted from Mineral Armor + Glossy Gemstones |

### Strategy

- Event starts at **6PM in-game time** (XX:30 UTC in real life) — different from most events
- Warning messages appear 55 and 35 real-life minutes before (sent at 2.75 and 1.75 SkyBlock days prior)
- Best used for grinding Mining skill XP alongside double drops
- Prioritize Crystal Hollows for double gemstone drops + Glossy Gemstone collection
- Living Metal Heart is NOT affected by this event

## 4. Dark Auction

**Timing:** Every 3 SkyBlock days (every 1 real-life hour), at midnight SkyBlock time. In real life, this is ~5 minutes before the hour (1:55, 2:55, 3:55, etc.).

### Access Requirements

- Minimum 400,000 coins in purse to enter
- Sirius appears at Wilderness coordinates (91, 75, 176) at midnight for 35 seconds
- Click Sirius to enter a queue — only the richest players among those queuing are admitted
- Limited server size: 12 players per lobby (reduced from 20 in Jan 2026)

### Auction Mechanics

- 45 seconds prep after entering, then Sirius rains invisibility potions
- 5 items auctioned per standard DA; 8 items during **Darker Auctions** (when Scorpius is mayor)
- Each auction: item shown for 5 seconds, bidding opens for 10 seconds — timer resets on each bid
- Bids are in fixed increments (click items on the bidding line); cannot bid lower than current high bid
- Coins are immediately removed on bid, refunded if outbid
- If you disconnect while winning high bid: coins are deducted, item sent to Jerry NPC

## 5. New Year Celebration

**Timing:** Late Winter 29-31 (lasts 3 SkyBlock days / ~1 hour real time, every ~124 real-life hours)

### Mechanics

The Baker NPC appears in the events area behind Hub spawn.

- **Free New Year Cake:** Claim one cake per event from the Baker (one per SkyBlock year)
- **New Year Cake Bag:** Purchase from Baker for 250,000 coins — holds up to 54 unique cakes, grants **+1 Max HP per unique cake stored**
- Filling the bag from scratch requires collecting one cake per event for 279 real-life days (~40 weeks)

On Stranded profiles, the Baker appears on your Private Island instead.

### Strategy

- This is the only event that was NOT cancelled during Dante's reign
- Consistent passive benefit: prioritize collecting every year for the HP bonus
- Each unique cake in the bag grants +1 Max HP — cakes are numbered by year so duplicates don't stack

## 7. Diana / Mythological Ritual

**Timing:** Active for the entire duration of Diana's mayoral term (~124 real-life hours / one SkyBlock year). Diana is a regular candidate elected through normal mayor elections.

### Requirements to Participate

1. Diana must be elected as Mayor (or have Mythological Ritual as her minister perk)
2. Own a **Griffin Pet** (purchased from Diana for 25,000 coins when she is mayor)
3. Own an **Ancestral Spade** or upgrade (purchased from Diana for 5,000 coins + 32 Gold Ingots)
4. Griffin Pet must be equipped (held) while using the spade

## 8. Fishing Festival

**Timing (Marina as mayor):** First 3 days of each SkyBlock month during Marina's term — 12 festivals per term. Each lasts 3 SkyBlock days (~1 hour real time). Interval: 28 SkyBlock days (~9 hours 20 minutes real time).

Also triggered by Foxy's Extra Event perk (Late Summer 22-24) or Mayor Jerry's Perkpocalypse rotating to Marina perks.

### Mechanics

Sharks are added to the catchable sea creature pool during the festival. **Shark Bait** (+20% shark catch chance) can be crafted from Shark Fins. Fishing Festival starts at **6AM in-game time** (XX:30 UTC in real life).

## 9. Mayor Election

**Timing:**
- Election Booth opens: Late Summer Day 27 (every SkyBlock year)
- Election ends / new mayor takes office: Late Spring Day 27 (following SkyBlock year)
- One full election cycle = 1 SkyBlock year (~124 real-life hours)

### How Elections Work

1. Five candidates are revealed when the booth opens (Late Summer 27)
2. Players enter the Election Room via the teleport pad in the Community Center
3. Each player gets one vote per account (NOT per profile); vote weight increases with Fame Rank
4. Voter's Badge and its upgrades increase votes cast; Artifact of Control doubles vote weight
5. Players may change their vote while the booth is open
6. The candidate with the most votes becomes Mayor; runner-up becomes Minister (their minister perk activates alongside the Mayor's perks)
7. Neither Mayor nor Minister can participate in the following election (exception: Diaz with Long Term Investment)

### Perk System

- Regular candidates start with 1 perk in their first election
- Each subsequent election they appear in: 50% chance to gain an additional perk (100% if they didn't gain one last time)
- Up to 4 perks maximum for regular candidates
- One perk is randomly designated as the "minister perk" (active even as minister)

### Special Candidates

Special candidates appear every 8 SkyBlock years (when year is divisible by 8), always with all perks. Current special candidates rotate in sequence:

| Candidate | Special Feature |
|---|---|
| Derpy | Doubles skill XP, halves prices, other chaotic effects |
| Jerry (Candidate Jerry) | Perkpocalypse — randomly borrows perks from other mayors |
| Scorpius | Darker Auctions (8-item Dark Auctions with expanded rare item pool) |

### Regular Candidates (Notable)

| Mayor | Key Perks |
|---|---|
| Diana | Mythological Ritual (Griffin Burrows), Pet XP boosts |
| Marina | Fishing Festival (12x per term, sharks), fishing stats |
| Cole | Mining Fiesta (5x per term), mining stats |
| Foxy | Extra Event (bonus of any other event), other quality-of-life |
| Finnegan | GOATed (expands Farming Contest brackets by up to 10%) |
| Diaz | Stonk Exchange, Long Term Investment |

### Dante (Special One-Time Mayor)

During Dante's reign, ALL scheduled calendar events (Spooky Festival, Season of Jerry, Traveling Zoo) were cancelled. Regular elections were replaced by "Dante Elections" (all 5 candidates were Dante clones — any vote went to all candidates, resulting in 100% Dante).

### Strategy

- Vote early: vote weight is what matters, not timing
- Increase Fame Rank by spending Bits and Gems in the Community Shop for more vote weight
- Track the special candidate rotation — Scorpius years dramatically increase Dark Auction value
- During Diana terms: prepare Griffin Pet and materials before election ends
- During Marina terms: stockpile fishing gear for 12 Fishing Festivals

**Timing:** Every SkyBlock day (every 1 real-life hour). Lasts 20 minutes real time. Located in Galatea (Murkwater Loch / Moonglade Marsh area).

Note: The events page refers to this as "Starlyn Contest" — Agatha is the NPC who hosts it.

### Agatha's Shop (Milestone Progression)

Milestones unlocked by accumulating Agatha's Coupons:

| Milestone | Coupons Required | Milestone Rewards | New Shop Items |
|---|---|---|---|
| I | 0 | — | Agatha's Power (Sweep %), Fig/Mangrove Fortune, Fig Personal Best, Common Frog Pet, Moonglade Jewel/Talisman |
| II | 50 | 2,500 Foraging XP, 100 Forest Essence, +2 Foraging Fortune, 5 SkyBlock XP, 50% Forest Whispers buff | Crow Shard, Small Frog Treat |
| III | 500 | 10,000 Foraging XP, 250 Forest Essence, +2 Foraging Fortune, 5 SkyBlock XP, 100% Forest Whispers buff | Mangrove Personal Best, Medium Frog Treat, Forest Pledge III book, Moonglade Ring |
| IV | 1,500 | 20,000 Foraging XP, 500 Forest Essence, +2 Foraging Fortune, 5 SkyBlock XP, 150% Forest Whispers buff | +1 Foraging Level Cap, Heron Shard, Large Frog Treat, Fighting Booster |
| V | 5,000 | 30,000 Foraging XP, 1,000 Forest Essence, +2 Foraging Fortune, 5 SkyBlock XP, 200% Forest Whispers buff | Giant Frog Treat, Moonglade Artifact, Shiniest |

**Moonglade Accessory Progression:** Moonglade Talisman → Moonglade Ring → Moonglade Artifact (each upgrades from the previous + Agatha's Coupons)

### Strategy

- Contests run every hour — consistent daily participation compounds Agatha's Coupon gains
- Prioritize getting to Milestone II (50 coupons) quickly for the Crow Shard
- Stack multiple point sources: cut trees during hunting sweeps for both Tree Gift and hunting points
- The Moonglade Artifact is the long-term goal for its Sweep stats
- Milestone IV grants +1 Foraging Level Cap (significant for endgame Foraging)
- Avoid Mangrove Trees until you have at least 50 Sweep stat (Agatha will warn you)
